What is a mortgage?

A mortgage is the amount of money you took out in loans to pay for the cost of a house that you are required to pay back monthly for a set period of time. Think of it as a fancy word for saying “house loan.”

What is a downpayment?

A down payment is the amount of money you have that you can pay for the house without taking out a loan. In other words, it’s the amount of cash you have in your bank that you can use to pay for the house before taking out a mortgage to pay for the remaining cost of the house.

What is a mortgage rate?

A mortgage rate is the interest rate charged on your home loan.

What is the repayment period?

The repayment period is the total time length you are expected to be able to pay off your entire mortgage by. This number can vary, but the most common numbers are 15 years and 30 years.
